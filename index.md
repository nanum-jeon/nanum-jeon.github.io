I am a Ph.D. candidate in Sociology at UCLA, with concurrent M.S. training in Statistics and Data Science. My research examines **life-course inequality in work and family**, using methods from **causal inference and machine learning**.

My work has been supported by fellowships and recognized with an ASA Student Paper Award honorable mention.
